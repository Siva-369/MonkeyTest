Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI Hiring Assessment Platform

How does the AI-powered assessment work?

Our AI-powered assessments use advanced machine learning algorithms to generate dynamic, non-repetitive job scenarios that adapt to candidate responses in real-time. The system analyzes responses for technical accuracy, problem-solving approach, communication skills, and more, providing a comprehensive evaluation of each candidate.

Can I customize assessments for specific roles?

Yes, all assessments are fully customizable. You can specify the job role, required skills, experience level, and assessment difficulty. Our AI will generate relevant scenarios and questions tailored to your specific requirements, ensuring you evaluate the exact skills needed for your open positions.

How accurate are the AI assessment results?

Our AI assessment system has been trained on millions of data points and validated against human evaluators with a 92% correlation rate. The system continuously improves through machine learning, ensuring high accuracy and reliability in candidate evaluations while eliminating human bias.

How long does a typical assessment take?

Assessment duration varies based on complexity and type. Technical assessments typically take 45-90 minutes, while communication and behavioral assessments range from 30-60 minutes. Our adaptive technology ensures candidates spend time only on relevant questions, optimizing the assessment experience.

Is the platform secure and compliant with privacy regulations?

Yes, our platform is built with security and privacy as top priorities. We are GDPR and CCPA compliant, use end-to-end encryption for all data, and implement strict access controls. Candidate data is stored securely and only used for the specific assessment purposes you authorize.

Can I integrate this platform with my existing ATS or HRIS?

Absolutely. Our platform offers seamless integration with most popular Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and Human Resource Information Systems (HRIS) through our API. We also provide dedicated support for custom integrations to ensure a smooth workflow with your existing recruitment tools.
